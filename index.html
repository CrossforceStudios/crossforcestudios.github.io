Crossforce Studios is a games studio that makes Seas of War - a turn-based strategy game that redefines the MUD atmosphere using Evennia, Django, and many game design tricks.

Seas of War (Server)

Want to host or make your own MUSE game? The official Seas of War game server is the way to go! Powered by Evennia, this project adds the necessary elements needed to play Seas of War or even make other MUSE games based on this (i.e. games with spaceships).
